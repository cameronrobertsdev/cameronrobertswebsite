My Work

App Design

After graduating with a bachelor's in media arts, I had the unique opportunity to work with Steve Halliday at Hothouse Enterprises, helping to design prototypes for innovative mobile applications.

Other Work

I have also worked on various other media projects including 3D motion design, short film production, and videography work.
